Latitude vs. Humidity

This graph shows the relationship between geographic location and the relative humidity on January 5th 2017. There is one conclusion one can draw from the presented information.

The first thing that you can see there is no correlation between these variables. Which is to say that while the relative humidity of a city will be influenced by its location, it is unlikely to be influenced by their relative posistion north or south. Instead it has much more to do with how close that city is to a body of water.
